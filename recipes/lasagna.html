Lasagna

Description

Instructions on how to make a delicious and hearty vegetable lasagna

Quick and easy recipe for any beginner cook to follow along

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 oz) package lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 3/4 cup chopped green bell pepper
  • 3/4 cup chopped onion
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 (26 oz) jars pasta sauce
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 (15 oz) container part-skim ricotta cheese
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook noodles in a large pot of boiling water for 10 minutes, or al dente. Rinse with cold water and drain
  2. Heat oil in a large saucepan. Add mushrooms, green peppers, onion, and garlic; Cook and stir until tender (about 5 min); Stir in pasta sauce and basil, and bring to a boil; Reduce heat and simmer for 15 min.
  3. While the sauce is simmering, mix together ricota, 2 cups mozzarella cheese, and eggs in a medium bowl.
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Spread 1 cup cooked tomato and vegetable sauce into the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Lay down 1/2 of the lasagna noodles and layer 1/2 each of the ricotta mix, sauce, and parmesan cheese on top. Repeat layering again with noodles, ricotta mix, sauce, and parmesan cheese. Top with remaining 2 cups mozzarella.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven uncovered for 40 min. Let stand for 15 min before serving.
